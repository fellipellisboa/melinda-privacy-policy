Effective Date: 16/05/2025

Melinda does not collect, store, share, or process any personal or non-personal data from users.

We do not collect:

Since Melinda does not collect any data, there is no data processing of any kind. The app does not interact with servers, databases, or external platforms for the purpose of storing or handling user information.
